## About the Internship

Join our content team as a Content Writing Intern and learn how to create engaging content that drives results. Work on diverse writing projects and build your portfolio.

## What You'll Learn

- Content strategy and planning
- SEO-optimized writing techniques
- Copywriting for different platforms and audiences
- Content editing and proofreading
- Client communication and feedback incorporation

## Responsibilities

- Write blog posts, website copy, and marketing content
- Create social media content and captions
- Assist in content planning and strategy
- Edit and proofread content for quality
- Research topics and industries for content creation

## Duration & Schedule

- 3-6 months internship
- Flexible schedule (15-20 hours per week)
- Fully remote work option

## How to Apply

Send your resume and writing samples to internships@sharkcreativeagency.com with the subject line "Content Writing Intern Application".
