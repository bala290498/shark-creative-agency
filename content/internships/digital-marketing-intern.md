## About the Internship

Join our marketing team as a Digital Marketing Intern and learn how to create and execute marketing strategies that drive results. Work on real client campaigns and build your marketing skills.

## What You'll Learn

- Digital marketing strategy and planning
- Social media management and content creation
- SEO optimization techniques
- Analytics and performance tracking
- Campaign management and optimization

## Responsibilities

- Assist in creating social media content and campaigns
- Help with SEO research and content optimization
- Support campaign management and monitoring
- Analyze campaign performance and create reports
- Contribute to marketing strategy discussions

## Duration & Schedule

- 3-6 months internship
- Flexible schedule (15-20 hours per week)
- Fully remote work option
