## About the Internship

Join our design team and learn from experienced designers while working on real projects. This internship will help you develop your design skills and build a professional portfolio.

## What You'll Learn

- User-centered design principles and methodologies
- Industry-standard design tools (Figma, Adobe Creative Suite)
- Creating wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- User research and usability testing
- Design systems and style guides
- Collaborating with developers and clients

## Responsibilities

- Assist in creating designs for web and mobile applications
- Participate in design reviews and feedback sessions
- Learn and apply design best practices
- Contribute to design system development
- Support the design team on various projects

## Benefits

- Hands-on experience with real design projects
- Mentorship from experienced designers
- Portfolio-building opportunities
- Access to design tools and resources
- Potential for full-time employment after internship

## How to Apply

If you're interested in this internship, please send your resume and portfolio to internships@sharkcreativeagency.com with the subject line "Internship Application for UI/UX Design Intern".
