## About the Internship

Join our design team as a UI/UX Design Intern and learn how to create user-centered designs that solve real problems. Work alongside experienced designers on client projects.

## What You'll Learn

- User research and usability testing methods
- Design system creation and maintenance
- Prototyping and interaction design
- Client presentation and communication
- Collaboration with developers and marketers

## Responsibilities

- Assist in creating wireframes and high-fidelity designs
- Participate in user research and testing sessions
- Help maintain design systems and component libraries
- Create design documentation and specifications
- Contribute to design critiques and brainstorming sessions

## Duration & Schedule

- 3-6 months internship
- Flexible schedule (15-20 hours per week)
- Hybrid work option (remote + occasional in-person)

## How to Apply

Send your resume and portfolio to internships@sharkcreativeagency.com with the subject line "UI/UX Design Intern Application".
