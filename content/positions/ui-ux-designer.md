## About the Role

We're looking for a talented UI/UX Designer to join our creative team. You'll be responsible for designing intuitive, beautiful interfaces that users love and that drive business results.

## Key Responsibilities

- Create user-centered designs for web and mobile applications
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate with developers to ensure design implementation
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Work with clients to understand their needs and translate them into designs
- Maintain and evolve design systems and style guides

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Flexible remote and hybrid work options
- Opportunities to work on diverse, exciting projects
- Access to the latest design tools and resources
- Collaborative team environment with creative freedom
