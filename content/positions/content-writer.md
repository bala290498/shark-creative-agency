## About the Role

Join our content team as a Content Writer and help tell our clients' stories through engaging and effective copy. You'll work on a variety of content types across different industries.

## Key Responsibilities

- Write blog posts, website copy, and marketing content
- Create social media content and campaigns
- Develop content strategies aligned with business goals
- Edit and proofread content for quality and consistency
- Collaborate with marketing and design teams
- Optimize content for SEO and conversions

## What We Offer

- Competitive hourly rate or project-based compensation
- Flexible remote work schedule
- Diverse writing projects across industries
- Opportunity to build your portfolio
- Supportive and collaborative team environment
